TV Submission

You can submit a flyer advertising your event or organization to be displayed on CEAS Tribunal TVs. These TVs are wall-mounted in Baldwin Hall and the Engineering Research Center. Your advertisement will be displayed alongside other promotional materials for 20 seconds and will continue to loop with the rest of the content. The advertisement will be taken down after 2 weeks or when the date of the event expires.

To submit an ad, please fill out the form below. Provide a brief description describing the nature of your advertisement.

Any advertisement regardless of the topics allowed will be reviewed according to the discretion of CEAS Tribunal. Please allow a week to review your submission. You will receive an email when the submission is approved.
